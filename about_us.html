About Imagiseum

Imagiseum is the first museum for people who love AI-generated abstract art. We celebrate the strange, the dreamlike, and the unexpected.

Our mission

We explore creative collaboration between humans and algorithms. The artworks here are experimental and aim to expand what beauty can be.
